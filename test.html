🔐 About Phishing Shield

Thesis: Friction is a feature, not a bug. By adding 2-3 seconds of analysis and a warning popup, we force users to THINK before clicking on suspicious links.

How it works:

  • Detects typosquatting (paypa1 vs paypal)
  • Checks for suspicious patterns (IP addresses, URL shorteners, etc.)
  • Flags phishing keywords in URLs
  • Shows beautiful warning with risk analysis
  • Tracks statistics in real-time
